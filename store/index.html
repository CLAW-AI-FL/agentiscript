Common questions

Is the free version actually free?

Yes. CC0. Do whatever you want. No attribution, no sign-up, no catch. The Pro pack exists to fund ongoing development — not to gate the basics.

Can I use the free SVGs in a commercial product?

Yes. CC0 means you can use it in anything — commercial, personal, open source. No credit needed.

What formats does Pro include?

SVG (scalable), PNG at 16/32/64/128/256/512px, and WebP at the same sizes. Plus a Figma file with every icon as a component.

How do custom icon requests work?

Enterprise customers can request up to 20 custom icons per year. Submit a concept and definition — we build it and add it to your private library.

Is there a refund policy?

If you are unhappy within 14 days, we will refund you. No questions asked.
